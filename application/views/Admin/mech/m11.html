Screw Head Styles 螺丝头型 & Screw Head Types螺丝批头分类的区别

Screws are the most common form of joining technology. Force is transferred to the screw by means of positive locking. For this reason each screw requires a counterpart fitting its profile in the form of a screwdriver, L-key or bit. Slotted, Phillips and Pozidriv cross-head, hexagonal and square head screw profiles are common. More recent profiles are TORX® and TORX PLUS®. Then there are additional profiles such as Torq-Set® und Tri-Wing®.
